# Roadmap (Project-Wide)

This roadmap is aligned to functional units FU-01 through FU-10.

## RM-01 Constitutional and Strategy Baseline

Scope:
- Finalize constitution-aligned service model.
- Establish strategy-to-execution target model and variance governance.

Exit criteria:
- FU-01 operating model approved.
- Traceability framework active.

## RM-02 Origination and Commercial Conversion

Scope:
- Deliver FU-02 lead/opportunity/quote/acceptance lifecycle.
- Ensure accepted commercial intent can trigger execution flow.

Exit criteria:
- End-to-end lead-to-order path works with proofs.

## RM-03 Execution Core (Product and Service)

Scope:
- Deliver FU-03 processing and FU-08 autonomy controls.
- Support product and service execution variants.

Exit criteria:
- Product and service scenario both complete autonomously unless policy requires escalation.

## RM-04 Product Operations (Inventory and Procurement)

Scope:
- Deliver FU-04 inventory and replenishment capabilities.
- Enforce AVCO-based valuation for product flows.

Exit criteria:
- In-stock and out-of-stock product scenarios pass deterministically.

## RM-05 Finance, Accounting, and Settlement

Scope:
- Deliver FU-05 AR/AP/GL and settlement integrity.
- Ensure deterministic postings for product and service flows.

Exit criteria:
- Journals balance and reconcile for every closed transaction.

## RM-06 Governance and Policy Controls

Scope:
- Deliver FU-07 approvals/escalations/freeze.
- Integrate policy checks into all execution paths.

Exit criteria:
- Governance gates can block, approve, and freeze actions with audit trail.

## RM-07 Reporting, Revenue, and Variance Management

Scope:
- Deliver FU-06 reporting with target vs actual and forecast variance.
- Produce board pack with financial and operational KPIs.

Exit criteria:
- Board pack reflects KPI, forecast, and variance governance decisions.

## RM-08 Audit and Evidence Readiness

Scope:
- Deliver FU-09 replay and evidence packaging.
- Ensure complete traceability across commitments, obligations, proofs, settlements.

Exit criteria:
- End-to-end transaction evidence export is complete and reproducible.

## RM-09 Agent Payroll and Unit Economics

Scope:
- Deliver FU-10 token/cloud/subscription cost accounting.
- Integrate payroll economics into finance and board reporting.

Exit criteria:
- Cost-to-serve and margin-after-autonomy-cost visible by period/workflow/order.

## RM-10 Demo and Production Readiness Gate

Scope:
- Run deterministic investor scenarios with no manual DB intervention.
- Validate resilience, observability, and acceptance criteria.

Exit criteria:
- All P0 requirements satisfied with evidence and traceability.

## Milestone Governance

Completion for every roadmap item requires:
1. linked requirements,
2. linked design specifications,
3. linked delivery tasks,
4. acceptance evidence artifacts.
